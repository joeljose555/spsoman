About Us

Who We Are

Group company was established in 1997 in KSA as one of the Pioneers in supporting major Oil& gas sector companies. Our knowledge and experience more than 25 years in the Oil & Gas industry helps to expand our network of Offices in UAE, KSA, Bahrain, Oman and USA. Fortune Oilfield was established in Dubai in 2012 and providing a single point solution and a supplier to Oil & Gas Industry in Middle East & Far East.

Variety of services now expanded to many sectors of Oil & Gas and Marine industry making us a “Single Point Solution” for Oil & Gas Industry such as: Fabrication of Accommodation Container, Tools Containers, MTU Skids, Man Lifting Basket, Gas Racks, Drill Pipe Baskets & Rig Mats with certification to meet the standards of different regions. Our Services extend to Bunkering services, Wooden Packing, Galley, Crude Oil & Product Tank Cleaning.

We represent several reputed American and European manufacturers and are in the process of diversifying further. Representation of world-renowned brands ensures the highest standards of product quality to our customers. Our strength is that we are an organisation of experienced professionals from the industry.

As one of the most diversified suppliers, FOGT can offer a wide range of supplies and services to *Drilling and Work-Over Operations *Oil Refineries *Gas & Petrochemical Plants *Power & Desalination Plants *Construction Companies. We are registered with most of the Oil & Gas companies in the UAE, Water & Electricity, Municipality, Dry Docks etc. Also, we deal with E.P.C companies and service companies.

Furthermore, we have joint ventures with reputed international manufacturers and service providers. We are quality driven and to emphasis our quality, we are accredited with ISO 9001:2015. We strictly follow the standards of ISO to maintain the quality and performance of operation and to make sure the customer satisfaction. Fortune Oilfield is also a member of IADC.
